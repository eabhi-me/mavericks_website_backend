Who We Are

Welcome to Mavericks, the official robotics club of Sant Longowal Institute of Engineering and Technology (SLIET). We are a community of passionate innovators, engineers, and problem-solvers who strive to push the boundaries of robotics, automation, and AI.
At Mavericks, we believe in learning by doing. Our team consists of students from Mechanical, Mechatronics, Electronics, Computer Science, and Electrical Engineering backgrounds, collaborating on cutting-edge projects, competitions, and industrial problem-solving.

Our Objectives

  • To promote creativity and increase the technical know-how and productivity of all students at the Institute.
  • To promote hands-on and co-operative learning, engage students in problem-solving, and work on Industrial Oriented Projects.
  • We inspire students to transform ideas into startups and research projects by providing mentorship, networking, and industry exposure.
  • Mavericks is a hub where students from different engineering disciplines collaborate, experiment, and grow together in a fun and innovative environment.

Our Achievements

  • Won in Smart India Hackathon (SIH) in 2019, 2020.
  • Two teams qualified for the zonal rounds of DRDO's Unmanned Vehicle Challenge.
  • Top teams in the E-YANTRA 2017, 2018, 2019 competition by Ministry of Education and IIT Bombay.
  • Glorious participation at technical fests at IIT Bombay, IIT Kanpur, IIT Delhi, IIT BHU, PEC, Thapar University, and GNDU Amritsar.
